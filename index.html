About me

Madli Uutma

People frequently make important judgments, such as which political party to vote for, based on intuition rather than on a careful examination of evidence. This is often due to the decisions being time consuming. Through clever visualizations and analysis, we can easily make good decisions based on data.

On this webpage, I have listed some of my experimentations with gathering insights from data. As my background is in Experimental Psychology and Law, I especially enjoy using data to discover patterns in human behavior and use data for a societal cause. I am currently studying Data Science at the University of Amsterdam.
