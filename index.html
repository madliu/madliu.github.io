About me

Madli Kivisik

People frequently make important judgments based on intuition rather than on a careful examination of evidence. This is often due to the decisions being time consuming. Through clever visualizations and analysis, we can easily make good decisions based on data.

On this webpage, I have listed some of my experimentations with gathering insights from data, as a data scientist in IBM, product manager at Pactum but also outside of work. As my background is in Experimental Psychology and Law, I especially enjoy using data to discover patterns in human behavior and use data for making the world more awesome.
